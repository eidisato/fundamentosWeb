Kaike "KSCERATO" Cerato (formerly stylized as kscerato) (born September 12, 1999) is a Brazilian professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player for FURIA Esports. He is Kauan "KNCERATO" Cerato's brother and Adriano "WOOD7" Cerato's cousin.
